What is CBM?

CBM stands for "Cubic Meter" and is the unit of measurement for the volume of your shipment. It helps determine shipping costs and container space requirements.

Cubic Feet (ft³) is another common measurement used in shipping, especially in countries using the imperial system.

How to calculate CBM:

For Cubic Meters: (Length × Width × Height) ÷ 1,000,000 × Number of Cartons

For Cubic Feet: (Length × Width × Height) ÷ 1,728 × Number of Cartons

Note: Length, Width, and Height must be in the same units for accurate calculation.
